Your Grocery, Decoded.

NutriScan is a mobile grocery tracking app that turns your shopping cart into smart, simple, and healthy meals. It's designed to make healthy eating effortless and intuitive.

The Challenge

Healthy Eating Starts at the Store, But It's Complicated.

Most people want to eat healthier, but face challenges like confusing nutrition labels, impulse buys, and food waste from unused ingredients. There's a clear gap between buying groceries and turning them into meals that align with health goals.

Introducing the Solution

NutriScan makes grocery tracking effortless and intelligent. By providing tailored recipe recommendations based on what users buy, it removes the guesswork and calorie counting, turning a shopping cart into a personalized meal plan.

Core Features

How NutriScan Helps

Keep a Record of Your Groceries

Keep a digital log of your grocery items in one place. NutriScan helps you monitor what you buy, avoid waste by tracking expiration dates, and stay aligned with your nutrition goals.
